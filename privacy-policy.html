Information We Collect

Our Apps do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All user data is stored locally on your device.

Depending on the App, data stored locally may include:

Permissions are only requested when necessary and are never used beyond the stated purpose.

Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, sell, or share your personal data with any third party. We implement reasonable measures to ensure data stored on your device remains secure.
